What Is J2TECH Threat Monitor ?

J2TECH Threat Monitor™ empowers MSSPs of all sizes by reducing the complexity and cost of threat detection, response, and reporting. You get an all-in-one security operations center (SOC) that is unified, scalable, and affordable.

Compliance Reporting

Simplify your compliance efforts with detailed reports.

Alarm Engine

Designed to alert you to only relevant threats.

Log-Event Archive

Archive log events for up to one year.

What can you expect from J2TECH Threat Monitor ?

  • Threat Intelligence–Continuously updated from multiple sources, designed to help you find and respond to threats.
  • Security Information and Event Management – Collects log-file information and helps you hone in on the most critical threats by rapidly assessing intent and severity.
  • Advanced Log Search – Rapidly normalize, search, and analyze thousands of logs to help understand the nature of threats and attacks.
  • Log Correlation and Analysis – Threat Monitor correlates logs in near-real-time from your customers’ systems and analyzes them against multiple sources of continuously updated threat intelligence.
  • Automated Responses – Automate intelligent action steps to help remediate security incidents, reducing the need for constant user interaction.
